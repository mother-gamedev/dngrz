## The question this gate answers

> *Is the pitch/bat duel fun on its own — with no zones, no Phenoms, no factions, no momentum, no tactical layer?*

This is the v1 kill-switch. If the bare core isn't fun, no amount of system on top of it fixes that. Be honest here — the cost of a false PASS is building Milestone 2 on a foundation that doesn't hold.

**What's wired for this test:** field + ball + pitcher (PitcherAI toggleable) + batter (BatterAI toggleable) + HUD. **No fielders, no baserunning** — when the ball lands, the result is logged and the scene resets for the next pitch.

---

## Pass criteria (must hit BOTH)

### Criterion 1 — Specific moment feel
After 20+ at-bats: can you describe **3 distinct moments where contact felt _deserved_**? Hitting a well-located fastball *after* setting it up should feel weighty, crisp, earned. Whiffing should feel like *your* read failure — not input lag or hitbox roulette.

### Criterion 2 — Voluntary replay
Within a week of the prototype being playable, do you sit down to play **more than one session unprompted** (incl. solo vs AI)? If you only play when you've blocked time to "test," replay value is broken.
